Deep Rock Galactic Items adds a few weapons from Deep Rock Galactic. Each weapon can be upgraded to improve their abilities throughout the game. Upgrades are obtained by completed quests given by Mission Control.

New Content:
- 5 weapons
- 9 upgrades for each weapon
- 1 NPC
- 3 quest types
- 1 secret pet

Deep Rock Galactic is an IP of Ghost Ship Games.
